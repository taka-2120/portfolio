## Article 4. (Store Data)

Personal data and other analytical information (hereinafter referred to as "all information") are collected and stored as follows.

The data entered in this app is stored on the device or in an account linked to iCloud provided by Apple inc. (hereinafter referred to as "Apple").

For in-app purchases, the App Store provided by Apple is used. Purchase history and other information are stored in the App Store.

For user behavior analysis, crash analysis, and displaying ads, Google inc. (hereinafter referred to as "Google") provides Google Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, and Google AdMob. The collected information is processed for analysis and statistics, and sent to Google in a form that does not identify individuals. The developer uses crash information to improve the quality of this app.

In each third-party service, each company manages information based on its own terms of service and privacy policy.
